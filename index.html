    Campuses

    University Campuses

    The University of Azad Jammu & Kashmir is a multicampus and multi-discipline premier public sector University of AJK established in 1980. It consists of three campuses namely Main campus (Muzaffarabad), Neelum campus (Athmuqam), and Jhelum Valley campus (Hattian Bala).

    Muzaffarabad Campus

    The Muzaffarabad Campus is the main campus offering a variety of academic programs and modern facilities.

    King Abdullah Campus

    King Abdullah Campus offers advanced research facilities and state-of-the-art infrastructure for students.

    Jhelum Valley Campus

    The Jhelum Valley Campus focuses on delivering specialized programs to meet local and global needs.

    Neelum Campus

    Neelum Campus offers innovative programs in a serene and lead location conducive to learning.
